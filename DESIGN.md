# BalPOS Project Brief & PRD

## 1. Project Overview

**BalPOS** is a premium, enterprise-grade restaurant operating system designed to replace traditional POS systems with a modern, high-fidelity, and scalable digital ecosystem. The platform emphasizes operational efficiency, data-driven insights through AI, and a sophisticated "Forest & Cream" visual aesthetic.

## 2. Design System: Forest Enterprise

The visual identity is anchored by the **Forest Enterprise** design system:

- **Primary Color:** Forest Green (`#1A3A32`) - used for sidebars, primary actions, and brand accents.
- **Surface Color:** Warm Cream (`#FAF9F5`) - provides a soft, premium background contrast.
- **Typography:** Geist (Sans-serif) - chosen for its modern, clean legibility in high-density data environments.
- **Rounding:** 8px (Round Eight) - balances professional structure with modern softness.

## 3. User Personas

- **Restaurant Manager:** Monitors the Executive Dashboard and generates AI reports for business growth.
- **Waitstaff/Cashier:** Uses the POS Terminal for rapid order entry and customer service.
- **Kitchen Staff:** Operates the KDS to manage order preparation and flow.
- **Guest:** Uses the QR Table Menu for self-service ordering.

## 4. Technical Requirements

- **Responsive Layouts:** Desktop for back-of-house and POS; Mobile-first for table ordering.
- **High Contrast:** Essential for readability in various lighting conditions (bright kitchens vs. dim dining rooms).
- **Interactivity:** Fast scale-and-hover states for tactile feedback in high-speed environments.
